Blue Mountains Lodge

A fully equipped hunting base near Beaumont — available to NZDA members for overnight stays, hunt trips, and family getaways.

Location

  • 65 Rongahere Road, Beaumont 9591 — Open in Google Maps →
  • Approximately 1 minute south of Beaumont Bridge, on the left
  • Approximately 1 hour 20 minutes drive from central Dunedin via SH1 south
  • Look for a metal gate and cabbage tree at the entrance

Facilities

  • Main Lodge — log burner, electric ranges, refrigeration, microwaves, shower and toilet, TV & entertainment
  • 16-bed bunkroom — in the main lodge
  • 6 individual huts — 2 beds each, private accommodation
  • Meat storage facility — for processing and hanging game
  • Dog kennels — secure overnight dog accommodation

Booking Rules & Key Return

Keys must be returned within 48 hours from the last day booked (24 hours during April). Late returns affect other members.
  • Show your current membership card when collecting a key
  • Register all party members' details at the time of booking
  • One key fits all locks except individual hut locks
  • Leave the lodge clean and tidy for the next group
