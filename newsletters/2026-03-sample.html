Chairman's Note

Welcome to the first online edition of the Otago Deerstalkers newsletter. Going forward each monthly newsletter will be published here on the website, where it will remain as a permanent record for members and prospective members alike.

It's been a busy start to the year. The lodge had strong bookings over the summer and the range has had a great turnout on Saturday afternoons despite some unpredictable Dunedin weather. A big thank you to the range officers who turned up regardless.

If you have a hunt report, a story worth sharing, or a photo from the hills, please bring it to the next meeting — or send it through to the committee. The best newsletters are the ones written by members.

Range Update

The Chaz Forsyth Rifle Range is open most Saturdays 1–4pm, weather permitting. A reminder that June and July are maintenance closure months — the range will not be open during this period.

  • Eight benches available at 25m, 50m, and 100m
  • Members: $5 per session (current card required)
  • Non-members: $10
  • Hearing and eye protection are mandatory

Closure notices due to weather are posted on the Chaz Forsyth Rifle Range Facebook Group — follow it to avoid a wasted trip.

Competition Reminder

The annual photo and video competition year runs to 31 May. All photos entered must have been taken in the year ending on that date. Categories include general hunting/scenic, live game animals, human interest, and video.

Trophy entries and judging dates are confirmed at club meetings — come along on the second Monday of the month to stay informed.
