Annual Competitions

The Otago Branch runs annual competitions recognising excellence in hunting trophies, photography, and video — open to all financial members.

Photo & Video Competition

Photos and videos must be taken in the year ending 31 May. Enter across multiple divisions for your best chance of winning.

Be a Financial Member

Prepare Your Entry

Trophy entries should be brought along to the judging event. Photo and video entries should be taken within the competition year (ending 31 May).

3

Submit at a Club Meeting

Trophy judging takes place at an evening event announced at monthly meetings — typically mid-year. Photo and video entries close on 31 May each year. Entry is free for current members.
