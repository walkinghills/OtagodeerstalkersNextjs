Join the Otago Branch

Become a member of the NZDA Otago Branch and connect with a community of hunters and shooters across the region.

Why Join?

Membership gives you access to everything the Otago Branch has to offer — from our rifle range and backcountry lodge to club hunts, education courses, and our monthly social nights.

  • ✓
    Leith Valley Range – Members rate of $5/session (vs $10 for non-members)
  • ✓
    Blue Mountains Lodge – $10/night for Otago members
  • ✓
    Club Hunts – Annual organised hunts for deer, goat, tahr, and more
  • ✓
    HUNTS Course – Access to our nationally recognised hunter education programme
  • ✓
    Competitions – Trophy, photography, and video competitions open to members
  • ✓
    Monthly Meetings – Guest speakers, trip planning, and a welcoming community
  • ✓
    NZ Hunting & Wildlife – Quarterly NZDA magazine delivered to your door
  • ✓
    Advocacy – A voice for hunters and shooters on legislation and land access
🦌

How to Join

Membership is managed through the NZDA national website. It takes just a few minutes to sign up online.

1

Visit the National Site

Head to the NZDA Otago Branch page on the national website to access the membership form.

Go to Membership Form →
2

Complete the Form

Fill in your details and select the Otago Branch as your local branch. Membership fees are collected at this step.

3

What Happens Next

  • ✓You'll receive a membership card by post — allow 1–2 weeks
  • ✓Bring your card to the range for the $5 member rate
  • ✓Show your card at any key pickup location to access the lodge
  • ✓Come along to the next club meeting — 2nd Monday, 7:30pm, 53 Malvern St
  • ✓You'll also receive the NZ Hunting & Wildlife magazine quarterly
👋 New to the branch? Introduce yourself at the next club meeting — the committee and members are happy to help you get oriented, whether you're new to hunting or an experienced shooter looking for a community.
ℹ️ Membership is administered nationally by NZDA. If you have questions specific to the Otago Branch, attend any club meeting on the 2nd Monday of the month at 7:30pm at 53 Malvern Street, Woodhaugh, Dunedin.

What Members Get

🎯

Range Access

Use the Leith Valley Rifle Range at the members rate of $5 per session. Non-members pay $10. Your membership card is required at the gate.

Range Info →
🏠

Lodge Access

Book the Blue Mountains Lodge at $10/night (Otago members), including bunkhouse and private huts. Keys collected from authorised Dunedin retailers.
