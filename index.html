About the Otago Branch

A volunteer-run club for hunters and shooters in the Otago region — with real assets and a genuine community.

Who We Are

Based in Dunedin, we give members practical, tangible benefits: a rifle range in Leith Valley, a fully equipped hunting lodge in the Blue Mountains near Beaumont, and organised hunts into the region's backcountry. We are volunteer-run — if you hunt or shoot in Otago, this is your community.

Beyond the facilities, we advocate for fair firearms legislation, support hunter education, and run annual competitions that celebrate the best trophies and photography from the season.

  • ✓
    Advocacy for hunting access and firearms rights
  • ✓
    Promotion of safe, ethical, and skilled hunting
  • ✓
    Conservation projects and pest control
  • ✓
    Hunter education through the HUNTS programme
  • ✓
    Annual competitions recognising hunting achievement
