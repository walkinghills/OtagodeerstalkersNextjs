Chaz Forsyth Range

An outdoor range offering 25, 50, and 100 metre shooting distances — open to members and visitors most Saturdays.

⚠️ Weather dependent. Closures are announced on the Chaz Forsyth Range Facebook Group. Check before heading out.

Opening Hours

  • ✓
    When: Most Saturdays, 1:00pm – 4:00pm
  • ✗
    Closed: June & July (maintenance), Christmas period, around public holidays
  • ⚠
    Note: Arrive well before 4pm — the 10-minute walk from the road takes time.

Facilities

  • ✓8 benches
  • ✓Firing distances: 25m, 50m, and 100m
  • ✓Targets supplied on the day
  • ✓Range Officers available — happy to assist with sighting and technique

Mandatory Safety Requirements

🔒 Hearing and eye protection are mandatory for all persons on the range — including spectators. Bring your own earmuffs and eye protection.
  • ✓Remove bolt from firearm when not actively shooting
  • ✓Use chamber safety flags when benches are not in use
  • ✓Follow all Range Officer instructions at all times
  • ✗No dogs permitted on the range
  • ✗No smoking
  • ✗Small children are not permitted; older children must be under constant adult supervision

Fees

CategoryFee
NZDA Members$5.00
Non-members$10.00

Current membership card required for member rate.

Location & Access

  • 📍Leith Valley Road, Dunedin
  • 🚗Access via Leith Valley Road, or from SH1 at Leith Saddle heading south. Allow 10 minutes walk from the road to the range.
  • 🗺Open in Google Maps →
  • 🚶10-minute walk from the road to the range

What to Bring

  • ✓Ear protection (mandatory)
  • ✓Eye protection (mandatory)
  • ✓Membership card (for member rate)
  • ✓Warm clothing and sturdy footwear
  • ✓Your own ammunition
